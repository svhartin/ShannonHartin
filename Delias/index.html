Ten years ago, we began our journey along Hwy 27. Quickly, we became a favorite among the locals with our delicious authentic Mexican dishes. Delia's Mexican Restaurant has grown into three locations in surrounding areas of Chattanooga, TN.

We stand out with our limitless chips & salsa and over 50 + authentic Mexican dishes to choose from.
