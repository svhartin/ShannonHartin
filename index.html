I write about the messy parts of life, the beautiful parts of life, and the parts I'm still trying to figure out:

About motherhood and all the stresses, choas, and ways to make it through those sleepless nights. About the many battles, we as women, face. About overcoming those battles (negative self-esteem, stress, feeling unworthy) and becoming stronger. About taking on new paths and finding yourself. About personal experiences, like changing my career to learn front end web development, but in hopes to mix in social work and a little pazazz.

I write to advocate for women. I write to encourage moms to never start dreaming. I write to spread the word that moms can code too!
